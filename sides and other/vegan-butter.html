VEGAN BUTTER:  fluffy and creamy

watch here:  creamy, fluffy, vegan butter


ingredients

  • • 1 cup refined coconut oil (unrefined will give a coconut flavor)
  • • 1/2 cup neutral oil, such as sunflower oil
  • • 1/4 cup oat milk (or other non-dairy milk)
  • • pinch of salt
  • • pinch of turmeric (optional)


directions

  • 1. melt coconut oil
  • 2. whisk all ingredients together
  • 3. refrigerate for about 20 minutes
  • 4. whisk again with electric mixer until fluffy and soft
  • 5. transfer to butter container and place in fridge, or use right away
