CHICKPEA SALAD SANDWICH:  crunchy, creamy


ingredients

  • • 1 cup chickpeas, drained (save the liquid to make aquafaba for another recipe :) )
  • • 1/4 cup pickles
  • • 1/4 cup celery
  • • 2 tbsp vegan mayo
  • • 1 tsp mustard
  • • 1/2 tsp fresh dill
  • • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • • pinch of salt, pepper


directions

  • 1. mash chickpeas with fork or potato masher until desired consistency
  • 2. add in all ingredients and mix well
  • 3. scoop onto bread, toast, lettuce, crackers, etc. and enjoy
