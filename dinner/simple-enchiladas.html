SIMPLE BEAN ENCHILADAS


ingredients

  • • 2 cups cooked pinto or black beans
  • • 1 cup cooked corn
  • • 15 small corn tortillas
  • • 2 cups red enchilada sauce
  • • 1 avocado, sliced (optional)
  • • 1/2 cup vegan cheese (optional)
  • • small bunch cilantro (optional)


directions

  • 1. Preheat oven to 375 F.
  • 2. Pour 1 cup of enchilada sauce in baking pan.
  • 3. Spoon beans and corn into tortillas, leaving enough space to roll the tortilla. Roll tortilla and repeat for all. Line them in baking pan.
  • 4. Cover with remaining enchilada sauce.
  • 5. Sprinkle with vegan cheese.
  • 6. Cook for 15 minutes.
  • 7. Top with sliced avocado and cilantro.
