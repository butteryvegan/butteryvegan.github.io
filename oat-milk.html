OAT MILK:  super creamy.


ingredients

  • • 1 cup rolled oats
  • • 1/4 cup raw, unsalted cashews (soaked overnight or in hot water for 30 min)
  • • 4 cups cold water
  • • pinch of salt


directions

  • 1. blend cashews and water in high-speed blender for ~1 minute
  • 2. add oats & salt and blend for 10 seconds (don’t overblend, or the milk may become slimy)
  • 3. strain milk through nut milk bag and store in airtight container
